About me

When I was in highschool I realized I wanted to pursue a career in programming. My initial interest came with Excel and creating databases, something about figuring out the logic behind the calculations just drew me in. I didn't know exactly what it was or what field it was considered until I was introduced to Java. I immeaditaly took a great interest in figuring out how to put code together in order to display specific processes on the command prompt. While others in my class found it boring and useless, I knew that it was just the beginning and there had to be a bigger purpose. So I decided to go to college to study programming. I went into a degree program that focused on creating business systems. Where I learned the basics of C++, Javascript, Servlets and Applets. Due to many reasons I couldn't finish my studies and had to leave school. It's been a few years since then, but I knew one day I'd return back to studying programming, since I felt I had a knack for it and enjoyed doing it so much.

Being in college I worked on a lot of projects with others. Having the point of view of other people in the mix, not to mention ones who are as interested and excited about coding and developing like myself was really inspiring to create and push the limits of my skills to become even better.
